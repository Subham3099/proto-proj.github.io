Our Products

Here are some features of the product we offer:

  • Smart Irrigation Controllers

    These are electronic devices that help to manage the irrigation of plants by controlling the amount and frequency of water supplied. They can automatically adjust the watering schedule based on factors such as weather conditions, soil moisture levels, and plant type. This can lead to significant water savings and improved plant health.

  • Sprinkler Heads and Nozzles

    Sprinkler heads and nozzles are used to distribute water evenly over a large area. They come in a variety of sizes and shapes and can be adjusted to suit the needs of different plants and soil types.

  • Drip Irrigation Systems

    Drip irrigation systems provide a slow, steady supply of water directly to the roots of plants. They are particularly useful in areas with low water pressure or where water is scarce. They are also more efficient than traditional sprinkler systems, as they minimize water wastage.

  • Rain Sensors

    Rain sensors are used to detect when it is raining and to turn off the irrigation system accordingly. This prevents overwatering and helps to conserve water.

  • Soil Moisture Detection

    Soil moisture detectors measure the amount of moisture in the soil and alert the irrigation system when watering is necessary. This ensures that plants are not over- or under-watered and can help to conserve water.

  • Nutrient Content Detector

    Nutrient content detectors measure the nutrient levels in the soil and provide information on the health of the plants. This can help to identify any nutrient deficiencies and allow for adjustments to be made to the irrigation system.

  • Bluetooth Operable

    Bluetooth operable systems allow for the irrigation system to be controlled remotely using a smartphone or tablet. This can be particularly useful for busy homeowners or businesses who want to monitor and control their irrigation system on-the-go.

  • Android App

    An Android app can be used to control and monitor the irrigation system remotely from an Android device. It can provide real-time data on water usage and plant health, and allow for adjustments to be made to the irrigation schedule as necessary.
